Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ethereum staking? +
Ethereum staking is the process of locking up your ETH to help secure the Ethereum network and validate transactions. In return, you earn staking rewards in the form of additional ETH.
How much can I earn from staking? +
Current staking rewards are approximately 4.2% APR. The exact amount varies based on network conditions and validator performance.
Is there a minimum amount to stake? +
No, there is no minimum requirement. You can start staking with any amount of ETH.
Can I unstake my ETH anytime? +
Yes, you can unstake your ETH whenever you want. The unstaking process typically takes 1-5 days depending on network conditions.
Is staking safe? +
Staking is very safe. Your ETH is secured by Ethereum's proof-of-stake consensus mechanism. We use non-custodial staking, so you maintain control of your private keys.
