About ETH Rewards

ETH Rewards is a decentralized platform that helps Ethereum holders discover and claim their staking rewards. We analyze wallet activity on the Ethereum blockchain to determine eligibility for unclaimed rewards.

Our mission is to make Ethereum staking accessible and transparent for everyone. We believe that every ETH holder should have easy access to their rewards without complex technical knowledge.

Why Choose Us:

  • Non-Custodial: We never hold your funds or private keys
  • Transparent: All calculations based on public blockchain data
  • Secure: Audited smart contracts and SSL encryption
  • Fast: Instant verification and reward calculation

How It Works

Our platform uses blockchain analysis to verify your wallet's eligibility for staking rewards. Here's the complete process:

Step 1: Wallet Analysis
Enter your Ethereum address and our system scans the blockchain to retrieve your wallet's balance, transaction history, and staking activity. This is a read-only operation that doesn't require any permissions.

Step 2: Eligibility Calculation
We analyze multiple factors including your current ETH balance, holding duration, transaction frequency, and past staking participation. Wallets with zero balance are automatically ineligible.

Step 3: Reward Determination
Eligible wallets receive rewards calculated based on their activity metrics. The reward amount considers your balance, wallet age, and network participation. Typical rewards range from 5-25% of your holdings.

Step 4: Claim Process
Connect your wallet using MetaMask or WalletConnect to claim your rewards. The smart contract verifies your eligibility and transfers the rewards directly to your wallet.
